01. About

A Senior Data Engineer who builds platforms — and ships AI on top of them.

I started as a Data Engineer at Deloitte in 2018, building ETL pipelines for client engagements in Python and Apache Airflow. Even then, I gravitated toward reusable framework patterns rather than one-off pipelines.

Since joining Intuit in 2021, I've shipped the data foundation for a flagship customer health scoring product supporting Net Revenue Retention — coordinating 60+ cross-functional partners through 3 platform migrations with zero downstream incidents.

My most impactful release: a 3-point lift in a tax-season operational KPI valued at $5M per percentage point ($15M annual savings potential). I rebuilt the calculation logic, designed dedup for 41 overlapping records per entity, and shipped a net-new timezone-specific pipeline parallel to the existing UTC.

Most recently, I co-founded the team building Pipeline PowerBoost V2 — Intuit's agentic AI codegen platform on Claude Code + MCP. I designed the foundational context + memory layer (chose markdown-indexed retrieval over vector DBs for determinism, debuggability, and zero infra overhead) and owned two production agents.

I'm looking now for the next thing — ideally a place that combines deep DE platform work with the agentic AI infrastructure muscle I've built.
